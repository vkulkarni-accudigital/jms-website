Hardware Options

Two ways to track
every vehicle

Choose the hardware that fits your fleet's needs. Both options connect to the same powerful fleet management platform.

Option 01

OBD Plug-In GPS Tracking

OBD plug-in tracking devices connect directly to a vehicle's onboard diagnostics port, enabling installation in minutes without wiring or extended downtime. Ideal for growing fleets that require fast deployment and easy scalability.

Plug-and-play installation in under 5 minutes
Real-time location, trip history, and driver behavior data
Best for service fleets, last-mile delivery, and light commercial vehicles
No permanent modification to the vehicle

Option 02

4G LTE Hardwired GPS Tracking

4G LTE tracking devices are installed directly into a vehicle's electrical system, providing continuous power connection and enhanced operational reliability. Designed for commercial fleets requiring consistent uptime and deeper data capture.

Hardwired for continuous, uninterrupted tracking
Real-time location, geofencing alerts, and ignition status
Supports integration with cameras, sensors, and other fleet tech
Best for logistics, construction, and high-mileage commercial operations
