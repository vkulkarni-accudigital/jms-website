ELD Compliance Solutions for Commercial Fleets

Electronic Logging Device (ELD) systems help fleets meet Hours of Service (HOS) requirements while reducing administrative overhead and compliance risk. Integrated ELD platforms streamline driver log management, reporting, and audit readiness across regulated operations.

Core Capabilities

  • Automated Hours of Service tracking
  • Real-time driver log visibility
  • DVIR (Driver Vehicle Inspection Reports) integration
  • FMCSA-compliant reporting formats
  • Centralized compliance dashboard

Designed for Fleet Oversight

ELD systems are integrated into the broader fleet platform, allowing compliance managers to monitor driver status, identify potential violations, and respond proactively before issues escalate.

Selecting the Right ELD Platform

IFTA Reporting & Fuel Tax Management

International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) reporting tools automate the tracking and calculation of fuel usage across state and provincial lines. Integrated fleet platforms simplify fuel tax reporting by accurately capturing mileage data and jurisdiction breakdowns.

Core Capabilities

  • Automated mileage tracking by jurisdiction
  • State-by-state fuel usage breakdown
  • Quarterly fuel tax report generation
  • GPS-based distance calculation
  • Centralized compliance reporting dashboard

Simplified Multi-State Compliance

Automated IFTA reporting reduces manual recordkeeping, minimizes reporting errors, and improves audit readiness for fleets operating across multiple jurisdictions.

Simplifying Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance
