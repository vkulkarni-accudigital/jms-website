Feature 01

Road-Facing Video Recording

Forward-view camera systems provide continuous roadway documentation, capturing incidents, traffic events, and environmental conditions in real time.

These solutions support liability protection, insurance claim validation, and structured event review across commercial fleet operations.

Common in delivery, service, and transportation fleets where documented roadway visibility is essential.

Feature 02

In-Cabin & Driver Monitoring

In-cabin camera systems monitor driver behavior, distraction, and compliance in safety-sensitive environments.

When paired with AI-enabled event detection, these platforms help fleets reinforce safe driving practices, reduce risky behavior, and improve operational accountability.

Often deployed in transportation, utilities, and regulated fleet environments.

Feature 03

Multi-Camera & AI Platforms

Advanced camera platforms combine multiple viewpoints: forward, rear, side, and interior, delivering comprehensive vehicle visibility.

AI-driven systems enhance this visibility with real-time alerts for harsh braking, collision risk, distraction, and other high-impact driving events.

Designed for high-risk commercial operations including construction, logistics, and asset-intensive fleets.
